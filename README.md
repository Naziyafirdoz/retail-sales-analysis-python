# 🛒 Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes retail transaction data (1000+ records) to uncover sales trends, customer behavior, and business insights using Python (Pandas). The goal is to support data-driven decision-making by identifying key revenue drivers and patterns.

---

## 📊 Key Features
- Data cleaning and preprocessing (handling data types, duplicates)
- Feature engineering (time-based features & customer segmentation)
- Revenue analysis by product category, gender, and time
- Customer behavior analysis (age groups & high-value transactions)
- Data visualization using Matplotlib (bar & line charts)
- Exporting cleaned datasets and summary reports

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Electronics category generated the highest revenue**, indicating strong demand. The business should prioritize inventory and marketing for this segment.
- **Female customers contributed slightly more revenue**, suggesting an opportunity for targeted campaigns.
- **May recorded peak sales**, highlighting seasonal demand. Businesses can increase promotions and stock during this period.
- **Weekend sales are higher than weekdays**, indicating better customer engagement. Special weekend offers can further boost revenue.
- **Adults (25–45 age group) are the most valuable customer segment**, making them the ideal target for marketing strategies.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- Total Transactions: 1000+
- Total Revenue: ₹4.5L+ (approx.)
- Top Category: Electronics

## 🚀 Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory and marketing budget for Electronics category
- Launch targeted campaigns for Adult customers (high-value segment)
- Introduce seasonal promotions during peak months (May)
- Offer discounts and deals on weekends to maximize conversions
- Improve performance in low-sales months through campaigns
